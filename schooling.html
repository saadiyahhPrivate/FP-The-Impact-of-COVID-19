School Restrictions data

In an attempt to reduce the rate of spread of COVID-19 and to "flatten the curve", countries across the world have gradually mandated school closures.

The visualization below shows how varios countries progressively closed schools starting with China in early January. Other countries, namely Iran and Italy also closed their schools around February 22nd and 23rd 2020, with many other countries following suit thereafter.

Although some ountries recommended partial school closures in affected school areas (light blue), these recommendations soon changed into generalized nationwide school closures (dark blue)

Disclaimer: the dataset we used unfortunately did not contain data for all countries for all dates, and so some countries appear white thoughout due to lack of data. We are looking into finding a better dataset for this visualization.
